How it works ?



If you are an artist

you can browse the lists and select the materials you are interested in, and use them to create recycled artwork. Showcase your work in our online gallery and connect with potential clients. share your current projects, and receive user notes.

If you are a simple user

you can register for free. If you have items to donate, you can add them to the platform. Artists on the site can select these materials to create recycled artwork. Use our filter system to search for materials based on category, location and availability.
